Myka's Spotify Wrapped 2021

Every end of the year, Spotify creates a playlist of the Top 100 songs you were listening to throughout the whole year. I think it's really cool, because they put them up by ranking. The ranking is based on how many times you've played the song. I've listened to various songs by different artists. So, it was a bit interesting for me to see what my Top 10 songs were. Some songs that made it were unexpected, and some were to no surprise. Now, let's have a look of:

My Top 10 Songs of 2021

  1. BTS - "Butter"
  2. Agust D - “People”
  3. Agust D - “Moonlight”
  4. BTS - “Life Goes On”
  5. BTS - “Film Out”
  6. Crush ft. Devin Morrison - “Let Me”
  7. Agust D ft. RM - "Strange"
  8. Doja Cat - “Love to Dream”
  9. BTS - “Love Maze”
  10. Drake with 21 Savage ft. Project Pat - “Knife Talk”

As you can tell, BTS and Agust D are obviously my favorite artists. Interestingly, Agust D is a member of the boy group BTS. He is actually known as Suga in the band, but he goes by Agust D when doing solo projects (sort of his alter ego). I was expecting my summary to be diverse. The most surprising song for me to find on the list is Drake's “Knife Talk”. I didn't even feel like I listened to it that much. I thought I would see more Doja songs on the Top 10 because I was listening to her album Planet Her nonstop, especially all throughout the summer. But because this was based on the number of times I've played the song it makes sense that my Top 10 wouldn't have that much variety in terms of artists.
